Goal Focused Email Marketing Agency


Use our email marketing services to accelerate your business’s growth! The main goal of email marketing is to attract customers from all over the world and generate conversions. What we do is to send emails that motivate people to buy your products/services, and craft engaging newsletters to help you stay top of mind.

See What We Offer:

Website Call-to-Actions


Implementing call-to-actions (floating headers, website pop-up forms, sidebar forms, etc.) on your website is the first step we take in order to promote your lead magnets. This will contribute to converting website visitors into email subscribers.

Email Marketing Software


As an agency, we recommend specific email marketing software to be used, for example, HubSpot, MailChimp, or Omnisend. We will help you with selecting and implementing email marketing software to receive leads and send emails.

Advertising Campaigns


Once deciding the email marketing software to be used, advertising your lead magnets is the next step, and the fastest way to grow your email list! The lead magnets will be advertised on social media or search engines to generate more email subscribers. Traffic from advertising will be directed to a custom landing page where people will opt-in to your email list.

Email Newsletters


Here’s the final and most important step! To really grab customers’ attention and persuade them to purchase your products/services, we will design email newsletters that reflect your business. Blogs, case studies, events, and more will be part of the email content. We will apply best-practices for email deliverability, click-through-rates, and conversions.
