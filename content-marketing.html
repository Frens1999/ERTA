Content Marketing Services

Content Marketing Strategy


Crafting a unique strategy is the first step our content creators will take. By researching your buyer’s persona, we will learn more about your customers and this will help us write content that appeals to them. Besides this, we will develop a blog calendar that will allow you to see the topics we are going to write about and the date they will be published. All the written content will be sent to you beforehand for approval.

Content Development


The content we produce is professionally written, double edited, optimized with keywords, trending hashtags, emojis, call to actions, and it is published to your Social Media platforms and to your website. Content Distribution


Once the content has been produced, it will be published on different social media (Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Pinterest, etc.) and to your website. Last but not least is transparency and feedback. Each month, we will send content marketing reports in order to track performance and results. The main metrics we focus on are increased website traffic and time-on-site for each new user.
