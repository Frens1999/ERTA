Web Designs Services That Maximize Your Revenue


In today’s digital world, your website is the first place customers get to interact with your business. Almost 95% of a user’s first impression comes from web design. This is why web design services can have an immense impact on your company’s bottom line. More and more companies are reevaluating their website’s design and they are also partnering with ERTA Digital Marketing, the web design agency that’s dedicated to leaving your customers amazed when visiting your website.

Our Web Process

We will virtually meet to assess your needs and goals and give recommendations for how we can make a site that represents your business and functions well.

Wireframe

Together, we talk about the style that works best for your brand. From there, we make a mockup of your new home page as well as the proposed content structure.

Initial Development

After the wireframe process, we take that flat mockup and bring it to life with code, building out the home page and the skeleton of each internal page.

Demo

Once the website starts to take shape, we share a link so you can interact with it in real-time and give us feedback.

Final Development

For the rest of the development process, it is a collaborative effort. As we develop, you will be able to see the progress and do testing for yourself.

Launch

And voilà, your new site is live! Pop the champaign, prepare for your personal back-end WordPress training, and know that your site is in good hands while hosted on our private server.
