Social Media Strategy

Let's create a winning strategy together!


At ERTA, we are skilled in finding the right steps to overtake when planning a strategy. Pointing out the advantages of a strategy is easy, but pointing out the disadvantages of it is what we pay attention to.

Social Media Game Plan

It all begins here! Your game plan is a custom-made road map aimed to take your brand to the next level on social media. We will highlight your organization’s goals, challenges, and audiences, and match these with social media solutions. The outcome is a tailored strategy that works in harmony with your other marketing channels.

Content & Campaign Planning

Social Media continuously evolves and demands fresh content and posts regularly. For this reason, we use a structured brainstorm process to develop a high-level content strategy, generating actionable ideas for ongoing content and campaigns.

Social Media Brand Story

It’s crucial to share your brand’s story on social media to make the audience understand that there exist humans behind it. We can help you to refine and tightly communicate your value proposition by developing a tone of voice and visual style across social media.

Competitor Analysis

Before publishing content, it’s important to know what has not been published by others (your competitors). This will ensure you stand out from other brands in your niche! At ERTA, we do research to find your competitors, trending topics in your field, and the needs and wants of your audience.
