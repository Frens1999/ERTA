The Guide to E-Commerce Tools: From Mailchimp To Hootsuite

by Fioralba | Aug 21, 2020 | Digital Marketing

Before starting reading this article, think about these 2 thought-provoking questions:

  • 1. Are you using the RIGHT e-commerce tools to reach your full selling potential?
  • 2. Have you done enough Social Media MARKETING to keep customers engaged?

From eye-catching advertisements to shopping cart checkouts, there’s an UNending amount of areas to improve or simplify in your ecommerce experience. That’s why there are SO many helpful e-commerce tools to choose from! Wondering which tools to use for your ecommerce business? We aim to highlight some genuinely useful e-commerce tools to grow your brand!Let’s take a closer look at the best ecommerce tools for your business.

E-COMMERCE WEBSITE TOOLS

  • BigCommerce

If we’re talking about the BEST e-commerce tools, the list wouldn’t be complete without BigCommerce. What makes it special is their user-friendly method of e-commerce store creation and management. Want to know the best part?

You can easily drag-and-drop elements on to your webpage to develop your website. Additionally, it’s a completely hosted e-commerce store solution, which means that problem resolution is lightning fast!

  • Shopify

Shopify is perhaps the MOST popular e-commerce tool out there. Want to know the reason for its popularity? Read on to find out…

When you’re tech skills are minimal and you don’t have the resources to hire a web developer, Shopify and similar e-commerce tools are a good pick! It’s the wide range of options and customizations that Shopify offers that makes it a favorite among all ecommerce business owners.

It was also purely built for e-commerce! It’s due to this reason that you can find loads of options to manage your store such as inventory management, detailed analytics, and marketing solutions.

To help you with the tedious task of website building, it’s got a website builder as well!!

E-MAIL MARKETING TOOLS

  • OptiMonk

Before you start sending out emails to your target audience of potential customers, you’ll need to build an e-mail list (by following the steps mentioned in our previous article – How To Build An Email List From Scratch & 4 Email Subject Lines That Always Get Opened!)

OptiMonk describes itself as a more polite way to increase revenue by giving you a human-centric marketing tool! You’ll be able to create beautiful pop-ups that get the RIGHT message to the right people based on website behavior or even what’s in their shopping cart. It also connects with a number of other essential ecommerce tools, like BigCommerce and MailChimp.

  • MailChimp

Once you’ve got your list building tool set up, it’s time to send some emails. MailChimp is one of the BIGGEST names in email marketing for a reason: it’s great!

You can use MailChimp to create and send regular e-mail updates about your products to your list of subscribers and customers. You can also segment campaigns for different users and take advantage of email automation to save time.

Plus, you’ll get all the valuable insights that their analytics provide! There’s a reason SO many people trust MailChimp with automation, creation and promotion.

SOCIAL MEDIA TOOLS

There are quite a few tools that help you manage WHAT and HOW you post on Social Media channels (FB, IG, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Twitter, etc.). We’ve listed just the best of them down below!
  • Hootsuite
To build a STRONGER and more authentic brand presence for your e-commerce business on Social Media channels, you can use Hootsuite to schedule updates and interact with current and potential customers. With Hootsuite, you can watch what people are saying about your brand or products, schedule Social Media updates, and get access to powerful analytics and insights that can help you!
  • Canva

To drive more engagement on your Social Media channels, you can use Canva to create compelling images and graphics that relate to your products or business. Canva is a good tool to use if you have little or NO experience using a more robust tool like Photoshop!

Canva is set up with adaptable templates for e-mail, social media, website, ads and more. You can easily add fun graphics, fonts, pictures or patterns to create memorable marketing images for your products.

Canva is an easy-to-use design tool that almost everyone on your marketing team can work with!

CUSTOMER SERVICE TOOLS

You DON’T have a business if you don’t have customers!
Plus, a lot of other companies are fighting to make your customers THEIR customers!
It’s important that you figure out how to support them, advocate for them, delight them, and build up their loyalty to you!
  • GrooveHQ

GrooveHQ is a useful customer support product that offers a shared inbox for your customer support team, easy knowledge base setup and reporting that gives you the skinny on your company’s interactions with customers.

This tool is IDEAL for brands and retailers wanting to scale customer service to provide shoppers with better experiences and easier ways to communicate with your team.

  • LiveChat

This easy-to-use tool allows you to run a live chat on your website with minimal coding. Thus, you can answer customer questions and offer support through the buying process whether your customers are on a desktop or a mobile device.

LiveChat is on our list of essential ecommerce tools!

E-COMMERCE ANALYTICS TOOLS

Analytics tools use data science to help you understand WHAT your customers want, WHY they came to your store in the first place, and HOW you can attract more of them. Keeping an eye on these statistics is not a bonus in e-commerce; it’s a necessity!

The tools below make using that data easy even if you aren’t a statistician.

  • Google Analytics

To keep a pulse on how users are interacting with your pages and products, take the time to set up Google Analytics on your e-commerce website. With Google Analytics, you can track sessions, users, page views, conversion events, time on page, bounce rates, and more! You can also set up reports that can help you identify problems or opportunities in real-time.

Google Analytics is a must-have for any e-commerce business. If you aren’t familiar with the way Google Analytics works, Google offers FREE training to help you master this powerful tool.

There are many apps with better analytics, but Google is still the number ONE powerful analytical tool.
